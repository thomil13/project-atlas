If you're affected

First of all, I'm grateful that you're here, that you're alive. It's not easy dealing with an issue that is invisible to everyone except you. I know, I've been where you are, I know how it feels when your own mind turns against you. But there is help available, genuine help. Reaching out and asking for help might be scary, I've personally struggled with that for as long as I can remember. But it can also be a massive relief to finally talk about things with someone else, whether that's a friend, family member, or a mental health professional. This is not a sign of weakness, it is a sign of immense strength! I've gathered together a few links below, including one to a service called MyMind, the service I used to find my therapist.
