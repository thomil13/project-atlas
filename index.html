Welcome to Mind Yourself

Mental Health Tips from an Insider

Mental Health seems to be omnipresent these days, and rightly so. As someone who's been battling with mental health themselves, I've set up this site to provide help and tips that have helped me, not just for those who are suffering, but also for friends, family members and employers. I do need to point out that this is a purely private project. I cannot provide professional help, I'm not trained for it, and I do not have the skills for it. However, I've found that many of the sites and resources out there, whilst very helpful, were clearly written by "outsiders". I'm writing this from lived experience. Just know that help is out there, you are not alone and yes, you matter!
