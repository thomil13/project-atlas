Useful Links

Below, you'll find links to organisations and resources for anyone struggling with their mental health, either directly or indirectly. Do not hesitate to reach out to them.

Useful links for those struggling with their mental health

Talk Therapy

Tips on how to find treatment options such as couselling and psychotherapy by the HSE, as well as what to expect during a session.

Visit HSE
MyMind

Provids affordable and accessible therapy and counselling options across Ireland, both in-person and online.

Visit MyMind
Grow Mental Health

Grow provides group-based peer support groups throughout Ireland for those suffering from mental health issues.

Visit Grow Mental Health
turn2me

An online mental health service that provides individual and group-based support for people struggling with their mental health.

Visit turn2me

Useful resources for friends and family members

Mental Health Foundation

A useful guide on how best to support a friend or family member who is suffering from mental health problems.

Visit Mental Health Foundation
Shine

Shine aims to support and empower people with mental health difficulties as well as their families and friends throughout Ireland.

Visit Shine
Aware

Aware provides free support, education, and information services to people affected by mental health issues and their families.

Visit Aware
Mind UK

Mind has a wide range of supports available for friends and family members of those struggling with their mental health

Visit Mind UK

Useful Resources

IBEC

CIPD

Emergency Contacts

If you feel like you, or someone you care about, are in immediate danger of harming themselves or others, please reach out to one of the numbers below!

Call Emergency Services!

If you or some you know is at risk of physical harm, please call 112 or 999 immediately!

Samaritans Ireland

If you need immediate help, call Samaritans on 116 123, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year!
